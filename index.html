The Invention of Toilet Paper

Toilet Paper was invented by the Tang Dynasty of China around 800 CE. This invention had an incredible effect on the lives of the people, as it allowed for incredible productivity due to people being cleaner. However, soon after this invention, a group of purists called the ATPA (Anti Toilet Paper Association) declared a cultural war on the progressive toiler paper advocates. This conflict almost led to civil war, but eventually the ATPA was convinced of the positive effects of toiler paper and war was averted.
